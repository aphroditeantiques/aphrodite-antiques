Polished Antler Knives

A striking set of polished antler knives, handmade in Finland. The natural antler has been skillfully carved and polished, giving each piece a rustic yet refined charm. Signed with the word “Oulu”, the name of the city in Lapland, this piece carries both local heritage and artistry. The antler handles retain their rich natural tones, while the carving transforms them into functional art.

Dimensions: 38 cm wide × 25 cm height

Condition: Excellent vintage condition
