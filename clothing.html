A rare and exquisite pair of René Caovilla slingback heels, dating from the late 1950s to early 1960s. Crafted in shimmering Capretto Platino (kid leather), these heels embody the elegance of mid-century Italian fashion.

The shoes are presented in very good vintage condition, with light wear to the soles but uppers, straps, and buckles fully intact.

This set also comes with a matching gold evening purse, creating a rare and complete ensemble of timeless sophistication.
