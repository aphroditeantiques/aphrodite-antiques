Wayne Husted Antiqua Amber Decanter

The centrepiece of Wayne Husted’s work for Stelvia, the Antiqua line was known for its bold textures and distinctive embossed patterns inspired by medieval imagery. This amber decanter features the famous “Bird of Peace” motif, symbolizing harmony and artistry. The Antiqua pieces are highly collectible, reflecting a blend of American design, Italian craftsmanship, and peace symbolism of the 1960s.

  • Material: Pressed Glass
  • Pattern: “Bird of Peace”
  • Collection: Antiqua
  • Style: Mid-Century Modern / Danish / Scandi Modern
  • Designer: Wayne Husted
  • Origin: Italy
  • Date: 1960s
  • Dimensions: 27.5 cm high × 14 cm wide × 5 cm deep
  • Condition: Excellent vintage condition

Price: €280
